Instructor Training

Collaborative Lesson Design

The best lessons are built by a community of instructors. In this activity, you'll work with a partner to build a lesson using the techniques you've learned so far.

Made by the Mozilla Science Lab

Steps for the Activity (1h 30 min)

  1. Find a partner teaching the same topic as you. Using the concept maps and ideas you've worked on so far, work together to create a 10-minute lesson on part of your topic (30 min).
  2. Find another pair, and have each person take turns delivering the lesson they created with their partner. At each turn, have the presenter's partner film them, and the other pair act as students. Students should provide feedback after each lesson, using whichever feedback method they prefer. After your lesson, discuss with your partner how their delivery & interpretation of the lesson differed from your own (1 h).

Materials

  • Pen & paper

Discussion

After this lesson, learners should have some experience building a lesson with a partner, and seeing how multiple people interpret the same lesson plan, as well as some experience teaching a longer lesson.
